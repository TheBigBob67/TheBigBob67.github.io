Gas Prices Continue To Rise

By: Malikai Harrison

The states average price of unleaded gas is $2.77, but since last week it has risen to $3.15, a 38 cents increase.
While the average price of Eau Claires unleaded gas is $2.75, as of last week it has had a 38 cents increase making unleaded gas $3.13.

The rise in gas prices are an affect of the conflict with Iran, but some other reasons consist of higher demand for gas, the switch to "summer blend" gas, and rise in oil pricing. All of these factors are affecting the price.

One reason prices could rise is because of the higher demand for gas, and because Eau Claire is further from a major pipeline the trucking cost for the gas is gonna be more compared to Madison or Milwaukee.
